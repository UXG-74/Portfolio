KINETIK

Disruptor fitness experience.

KINETIK is a fitness experience built for ambition, with no drift from splash promise to dashboard proof. Goals, streaks, shared achievements, and progress clarity were shaped together, then iterated with AI until each touchpoint felt fast, social, and trusted.

Kinetik sits where wearable brands connect aspiration with readable telemetry. Targets called for journey traction across lived training weeks plus tighter IA inside the shell. Stakes stayed clear: steady hero clarity, confident thumb ergonomics, parity across onboarding, diagnostics, leaderboard context, pairing flows, recovery reads - credibility intact.

The constraint was deliberate: use AI as the creative accelerator - models to compress research and sharpen narrative, generative passes for mood and variants, Cursor with Claude Code for flows and microcopy - so quality came from iteration, not volume.

Performance software is judged in milliseconds - hierarchy, restraint, and spacing either read as confidence - or as chaos. Every module had to earn its place: hero promise, collections of proof in product detail, quiet states and failure paths, and dashboards that answer what matters now.

The stack was the studio: snapshot variants, prompt loops, microcopy drafts - all AI-native. Discipline was in direction - which routes shipped, which metaphors stayed, what still scans as intentional when someone opens the app before breakfast.

The outcome is a coherent product story captured in layouts and build handover: one voice ("KINETIK"), journeys from landing through logged-in ritual, and interaction patterns that respect hesitation in high-consideration analytics - without the experience reading like a slick poster beside an intentional shell.

OVERVIEW

Design language that carries from story to telemetry.

Kinetik spans two audiences inside one product instinct: fluent analysts and newcomers who sense effort before they decode charts. The brief wasn't “more hype” - it was to keep trust and momentum coexisting.

Strategy, UX, and storytelling were prompted, critiqued, and refined as one system so the voice could repeat without sounding repetitive. The goal was cohesion: the promise in the hero, the proof in KPI detail, calm orientation in onboarding, and reassurance where tension spikes.

THE CHALLENGE

A considered fitness product has to earn trust now.

This was never a template dashboard exercise. Discovery set the brief: define what people need to see, when they need it, and how quickly they can act with confidence under pressure. The challenge: shape clear IA and a component kit in Figma first, then use Cursor and Claude-assisted flows only to polish language and tighten the final experience.

Every pass followed client briefs: calm motion for legibility, modules for leaderboard, session, recovery, and onboarding, with plain language refined only after user testing.

SPEED & PRECISION

Instrumentation with clarity, before focus slips.

Training analytics is judged with emotion first - pride, rivalry, doubt - then justified with numbers: pacing, deltas, anomalies. The experience needed breathable rhythm: typography and spacing tuned for early-morning skim, terminology that sounds spoken, not pasted from a datasheet.

I treated scoreboard pacing as UX: restrained motion, generous gaps around ranked scores until dense graphs earn admission. Density stayed consciously opt-in - one voice bridging marketing heat straight into utterly forensic drill downs.

THE LAUNCH

Hardware realities, calm onboarding, with repeat visits.

Launch is where hype meets physiology. Flows assumed first-week onboarding, rusty returns, flaky Bluetooth firmware, and leaderboard comparisons at thumb speed - all without collapsing into legal footnotes at the scroll's edge.

Secondary reassurance sits where cognition spikes permissions, retries, restores. Documentation captured component boundaries so intent survives build - not a zipped mystery file. Elevate narrative, calm evaluation, give proof permission to steer the next action.

COLOUR PALETTE

Navy-steady. Emerald-electric - with Sky, Sunrise, and Pulse.

Primary stack follows the kinetic design tokens: Emerald (#02FB9B) for uplift and KPI wins, Navy (#121A2E) anchoring typography and dashboards, neutral NIKEL greys bridging density without washing out numerals - the same discipline as layering strain on a midnight UI.

Secondary accents dial Sky (#29A4F6), Sunrise (#FEC053), and PULSE (#FC5862) for alerts, rivalry moments, or coachable hotspots - controlled voltage, never confetti.

WORDMARK

Typographic velocity over decorative badges.

The identity leans on wordmark discipline - KINETIK set in a heavy Inter black - rather than an illustrated logo zoo. Supporting marks stay functional: stat callouts, rank chips, and wayfinding cues that appear only when motion or data needs emphasis.

Headlines carry kinetic energy in uppercase blocks; the neutral sans family carries numerals, micro-labels, and dense analytics so emotion lives in marketing rhythm while clarity lives in training telemetry.

OUTCOMES

Fewer narratives, sharper comprehension.

Research sessions converged around one storyline of value - not competing decks. Participants moved faster through onboarding and weekly check-ins once hierarchy and jargon were disciplined.

Product and engineering left with the same pragmatic picture of what ships next - component boundaries annotated, anomalies documented - so roadmap debates skipped theatre.

COLLABORATION

Documentation that mirrors the live promise.

Decisions lived in critiques, paired working sessions, and written specs - not surprise files. Build partners could implement without reverse-engineering intent; brand partners could see the same language applied in marketing and inside the shell.

Continuity from first touch to daily dashboard matters - when those promises align, trust stays strong in performance categories.

CONCLUSION

What this proves.

AI accelerates the messy middle - research synthesis, variants, KPI copy drafts, motion studies - but design still arbitrates tone, friction, truth. Kinetik is a case in disciplined iteration: fewer loud claims, more consistent signals, and a product world that invites return visits because it feels considered.

The live site and extended screens are the artifact - motion, campaign detail, and deeper narrative continue on the Kinetik website via SEE THE SITE in the band above.
